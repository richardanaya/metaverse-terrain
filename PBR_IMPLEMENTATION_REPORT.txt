TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS
================================================================================

Shader Architecture:
  Vertex: TBN matrix computation for tangent-space normal mapping
  Fragment: PBR lighting with GGX BRDF + hex-tiled albedo blending

Sampler Budget:
  Terrain: 12 samplers (4 albedo + 4 normal + 4 MRAO)
  Water: 3 samplers (1 albedo + 1 normal + 1 MRAO)
  Total: 15 samplers (within WebGL2 limits)

PBR Model:
  - Metallic-roughness workflow
  - Energy-conserving BRDF
  - Per-layer texture blending
  - Ambient occlusion modulation

Performance Optimizations:
  - Hex-tiling only for albedo (visual quality)
  - Simple tiling for PBR maps (performance)
  - Packed MRAO textures (3 channels in 1 texture)
  - Runtime PBR toggle (can disable for low-end devices)
